College of Engineering

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo

Building Future Engineers and Innovators

Mission

  • Provide quality engineering education.
  • Promote innovation and research.
  • Develop ethical and competent engineers.
  • Serve the community through technology.

Vision

To be a leading engineering institution producing globally competitive, innovative, and socially responsible engineers.

About the College of Engineering

The College of Engineering aims to equip students with technical knowledge, practical skills, and leadership qualities necessary to excel in the engineering profession.
